## Job description
=== TITLE ===
Flexible Housekeeping Job (Cleaning, Cooking, and Errands)

=== SUMMARY ===
- My wife (Rukma) and I (Nikhil) are a busy professional couple living in a 3-bed / 2-bath apartment near downtown San Mateo. We are expecting our first baby in November this year!
- We are looking for a part-time housekeeper to help with cleaning, housekeeping (e.g. organizing cabinets & closets), errands (e.g. grocery shopping), and light cooking
- We offer flexible weekday hours (ideally 5-10 hours/week across 1-2 days), and pay is $25-35/hr depending on your experience responsibilities 
- If you're interested, please reply with your phone number and your availability for a ~10 mins phone call. We look forward to finding the right fit for our home! :)

=== JOB RESPONSIBILITIES ===
- Cleaning: Clean floors, counters/surfaces, kitchen (+ dishes), bathrooms, bedrooms (+ linens), balcony, trash & recycling
- Housekeeping and organization: organize storage spaces (fridge, pantry, cabinets, closets); run dishwasher and laundry
- Errands: Shop for groceries and household essentials; pick up/drop off packages, dry cleaning, returns using your own vehicle
- Light cooking: Prep ingredients (washing, chopping, portioning); cook simple meals based on instructions provided

=== REQUIREMENTS ===
- Experience in housekeeping
- Good communication skills in English (spoken and written)
- Able to perform most job responsibilities and willing to learn new responsibilities
- Comfortable with technology/apps for task lists, grocery lists, etc.
- Owns a car for shopping and errands (preferred but not required)

=== COMPENSATION ===
- Estimated work: 5-10 hours per week, with potential for increased hours over time
- Flexible hours between Monday - Friday, 9 AM - 5 PM (ideally 1-2 days per week)
- Pay: $25-35/hr based on skill level
- Additional compensation for errands (mileage or gas reimbursement)
- Potential for performance-based bonuses
- Potential for additional hours and responsibilities in future (we will need a nanny soon)

=== APPLICATION PROCESS ===
1. Phone call interview (~10 mins)
2. Trial session (~4 hrs, compensated at $30/hr)
