- Types of keyboards / pianos
	- Acoustic piano
		- Grand piano: $20k+
		- Upright piano: $5k+
	- Digital piano
		- $500+
		- more bulky; have speakers which I don't need
	- MIDI keyboard
		- $100+
		- less bulky, more DAW controls; hard to find fully weighted
- Features
	- Digital piano vs. MIDI keyboard
		- Digital piano: more expensive;
