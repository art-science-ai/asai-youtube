## How did you get your career start?
When I started my career in 2014 as a Strategy and Analytics Consultant at Deloitte, many of our Fortune 500 clients were asking us to make sense of "Big Data" for their businesses. This sparked my curiosity to learn more about AI and ML to help my clients. I also shared my learnings internally through trainings, and externally by publishing a Deloitte Review white paper on AI.

I then went to grad school at Yale to study business, but focused my elective coursework on Computer Science and Machine Learning. Just when I was learning Natural Language Processing and Language Modeling, OpenAI launched GPT-2, and I was blown away by its possibilities and implications for AI's transformative impact on business and society. 

I decided to pivot my career to focus on AI, and I joined Adobe as an AI Product Manager.
