- üåê Meta's mission, products, and impact
	- As someone who grew up, lived, studied, and worked in 8+ cities across 4 different countries, my community of family and friends is spread far and wide. I intentionally use Meta's products such as Whatsapp, Instagram, and Facebook to stay connected with my various communities and to bring my world closer together. 	- Therefore I love that Meta is now one of the world's leading AI companies. I love these products, I love sharing these experiences with my family and friends, and I'm extremely proud to be working at the company behind these products.
- üè¢ Meta's culture
	- "Move Fast" (formerly "Move Fast, Break Things") is one of Meta's core values which is founded on empowering employees with trust and autonomy. In most companies your manager may say things like "can you please do this?" When I  joined Meta I was surprised to never hear those words. Instead my managers would say things like "Would you like to do this? It could be a good opportunity because..." always giving me the autonomy to shape my work. I often follow my manager's suggestions, but I also often choose to do other things that I find impactful.
