## Season 2 and newsletter launch

We’re picking up where we left off in season 1 and launching season 2 with fresh new episodes every week. Check out our intro to season 2 where you can learn more about the hosts and our plans for the podcast!

You can expect a weekly update including a new podcast episode and a newsletter article. Topics we plan to cover in season 2 include:

* **Where is AI’s iPhone moment in 2024?**
* **Getting started with ChatGPT**: advanced prompting, custom instructions, custom GPTs with a knowledge base and tool usage
* **Getting started with AI APIs**: automating AI workflows, optimizing your knowledge base with advanced Retrieval Augmented Generation (RAG) strategies
* **AI ecosystem and business overview**: AI ecosystem of GPUs, cloud computing, foundation models, operating systems, and applications; business models and strategies of companies like NVIDIA, Google, OpenAI, Apple, etc.
