### Recap of our 6-12-24 virtual sharing circle

Greetings, Fictionistas!

Thanks to the fab few who joined in on our summer kick-off chat yesterday. It was great to see some familiar faces, as well as a few newbies in the small, but wonderfully engaged, crowd.

The best part of the event was that EVERYONE participated by sharing their summer goals, projects, and plans, as well as the potential challenges they would face in trying to get it all done. 

Here are just a few of the things your fellow Fictionistas will be focusing on this summer:

* Finishing a novel (or two)
* Revising a novel
* STARTING a novel
* Developing a virtual masterclass
* House hunting
* Finding and joining an in-person writing group
* Establishing a sustainable daily writing habit
* Creating boundaries around that newly established habit
* Completing research for a non-fiction project
* Keeping the ball rolling on serialized fiction
* Hosting a podcast
* Writing X-men inspired fan fiction! ðŸ’œ
* Wrangling kids and maintaining work/family/creativity balance
