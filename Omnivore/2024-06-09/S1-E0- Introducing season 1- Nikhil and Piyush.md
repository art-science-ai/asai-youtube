### Art and Science of AI Podcast Season 1

Watch or listen to the episode on YouTube, Spotify, Apple Podcasts, Substack (this page), or copy the RSS link into your favorite podcast player!

In this introductory episode of Art and Science of AI we discuss our background, expertise, and motivation for embarking on this journey to demystify AI! Piyush shares his fascination with seemingly magical AI technologies like ChatGPT and MidJourney, and his desire to understand the underlying mechanics. Nikhil shares his background in AI, and his plan for demystifying ChatGPT in this season: starting with the basics of Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML), we will demystify key concepts such as neural networks, deep learning, and Large Language Models (LLMs). We will also explore the exciting potential of ChatGPT and its implications for business and society. Note: Season 1 was originally recorded back in May 2023 as a continuous 3-hour long conversation. Season 2 is now live, so please subscribe for new episodes every week!
