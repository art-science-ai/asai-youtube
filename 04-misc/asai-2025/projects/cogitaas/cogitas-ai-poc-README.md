# Cogitas - Nikhil AI POC
## Overview
- Cogitas is a consultancy specializing in expert statistical analysis for marketing organizations of large multinationals, focusing on areas like pricing analysis and marketing mix modeling. Recognizing the emerging potential of AI Agents, Cogitas aims to explore their integration into its services and operations.
- Nikhil, an AI product development expert with a focus on AI agents for productivity and workflow automation, is collaborating with Cogitas to support their exploration of AI integration.
- The collaboration with Cogitas focuses on two primary areas:
	- Education: Delivering a comprehensive talk/workshop on AI agents to approximately 20 company personnel.
	- Demo / POC: Developing a proof-of-concept (POC) AI agent tailored for their pricing analysis scenario planning use case.


## Scope of work

This plan outlines the engagement, with clear expectations and deliverables for each phase.

### **Phase 1: Foundation & Discovery**
- **Timeline:** Week 1 (Current week: starting November 24, 2025)
- **Name:** Foundation & Discovery
- **Description:** The initial phase to align on project goals, scope the POC, and plan the educational workshop.
- **Milestones and Deliverables:**
    - A document outlining the POC scope, use cases, and success metrics.
    - A finalized agenda for the educational workshop.
- **Expectations from Cogitas:**
    - Assign an individual(s) responsible for working with Nikhil and providing the required inputs 
    - Share documentation on current pricing analysis workflows, data, and tools.
    - Provide timely feedback on the POC scope and workshop agenda.
- **Expectations from Nikhil:**
    - Draft and finalize the POC scope and workshop agenda documents.
    - Establish the project management structure.

### **Parallel Workstreams (Weeks 2-5)**

#### **Workstream A: Education**
- **Timeline:** Weeks 2-3
- **Name:** Workstream A: Education
- **Description:** To educate the Cogitas team on the fundamentals and applications of AI agents in their business context.
- **Milestones and Deliverables:**
    - Live 1-hr workshop to cogitas employees (via Zoom / VC) delivered by Nikhil.
    - A recording of the workshop for future reference (if applicable).
- **Expectations from Cogitas:**
    - Ensure team attendance and active participation.
    - Provide feedback to Nikhil on workshop planning
- **Expectations from Nikhil:**
    - Create high-quality, tailored workshop content.
    - Deliver an engaging and informative workshop and Q&A session.

#### **Workstream B: POC Development**
- **Timeline:** Weeks 2-5
- **Name:** Workstream B: POC Development
- **Description:** Demo technical implementation of AI agent for Cogitas' pricing analysis scenario planning use case.
- **Milestones and Deliverables:**
    - A functional POC application (as a CLI or simple web interface).
    - A summary of test results.
- **Expectations from Cogitas:**
    - Provide comprehensive data, assets, and test Q&A for development and testing.
    - Be available for regular check-ins and feedback.
    - Estimated time commitment: 2-3 hrs per week
- **Expectations from Nikhil:**
    - Design, architect, develop, and test the POC solution.
    - Provide regular, clear progress updates.

### **Phase 3: Delivery**
- **Timeline:** Week 6
- **Name:** Delivery
- **Description:** To demonstrate the POC, hand over all project assets, and collaboratively plan for the future.
- **Milestones and Deliverables:**
    - A successful live demonstration of the POC to all stakeholders.
    - A project retrospective summary including a high-level roadmap for future work.
    - A written testimonial from Cogitas.
- **Expectations from Cogitas:**
    - Participate in the final demo and project retrospective.
    - Provide feedback and engage in a discussion about future opportunities.
    - Provide a written testimonial upon successful project completion.
- **Expectations from Nikhil:**
    - Prepare and lead the final presentation and demo.
    - Organize and deliver all project assets in a clear and accessible format.
    - Facilitate the retrospective and roadmap discussion.

## Project Management
- Weekly meetings will be held between Nikhil and one assigned person from Cogitas to discuss progress and provide updates on milestones.
