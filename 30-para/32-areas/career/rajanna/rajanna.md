## Professional Summary

V Rajanna is a senior executive at **Tata Consultancy Services (TCS)**, currently serving as **President of Technology, Software, and Services**. He plays a key role in driving growth and strategy for the company's technology and software divisions.

A veteran at TCS with over 32 years of experience, Rajanna has held various leadership positions, including heading the Telecom Technology Business Unit and leading TCS's regional operations. He is particularly noted for his leadership in expanding TCS's presence in **Hyderabad**, transforming it into one of the company's largest delivery centers.
