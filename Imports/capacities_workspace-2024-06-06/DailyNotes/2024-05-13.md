    - PM Skills: Stakeholder Management | [[../Pages/Cathy Huang]] Director of Strategic Initiatives

        - Intro

            - Cathy has been at Meta since 2010!

            - Stakeholder management is important, because Meta's culture is top-down decision making and XFN review heavy

        - Science of stakeholder management: hard skills

            - Define goals: what's the end result - decision, investment, sign-off, etc.

            - Define people: leadership stakeholders, leads, functional leads, working team

                - Get sign off on people list from key stakeholders

                - Ensure all stakeholders are sufficiently informed

            - Define process: timeline and work plan, communication channels to reach stakeholders

                - Explicitly clarify which communications go in which forums to which stakeholders

                - Build backchanneling into timeline

        - Art of stakeholder management: soft skills

            - Extreme clarity in communication: decisions needed, goals vs. non-goals

            - Showing confidence

            - Building trust and credibility
