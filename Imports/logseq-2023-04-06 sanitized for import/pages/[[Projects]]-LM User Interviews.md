	 - •Icebreaker
		 - •Thank you for signing up to participate in this interview. My name is [name] and I’m interviewing people to understand how and why people create and view PDF documents and what problems they run into

		 - •What’s your job title? Where do you work?

	 - •Intro
		 - •If you have not already, I would like you to select 2-3 PDF documents that you use for work or leisure. During the interview, we will ask you to show us the workflow you go through to create, view, and distribute these documents.

		 - •Please open a PDF

		 - •How have you engaged with this PDF? (Falling into the category of, [create/distribute, view], but keeping the question open-ended to learn more about what is possible)

	 - Creation
		 - •**Use Case**
			 - •Can you briefly describe this document?

			 - •Who is the intended audience for this document?

			 - •Do you have any particular goals or expectations for how people will use these PDFs?

			 - •How well are those goals being met?

			 - •What are the steps you take to ensure the readers can easily view/[perform other intended interactions for] this document?

			 - •How did you create this document? Please describe the process including which devices and applications were used.

			 - •How are such documents typically distributed to the intended audience? E.g. as email attachments, as links, or some other method?

			 - •Do you prefer the recipients to read the document in its exact current format, a format that is more easily readable by them, or something in between?

		 - •**Alternatives:**
			 - •Why did you choose the PDF file format?

			 - •Did you consider any alternative file formats? Why / why not?

		 - •**Problems: **
			 - •Things about this PDF that makes the use case challenging/serve its purpose (Open-ended, rate severity from 1-7)
				 - •How frequently do you experience [problem]?

				 - •What were the consequences of [problem]?

				 - •How severe is this problem?

			 - •Here are some challenges other users have found in the past, which ones do you resonate with? Do you have examples?
				 - •PDFs I create are difficult to view in Mobile

				 - •PDFs I create are not Accessible

				 - •…
