- Notes
	 - Adobe career development framework
		 - Be creative: Create What’s Next
			 - Look for new approaches and volunteer to take on challenges

			 - Make choices and seek clarity to ensure you can achieve results, especially under ambiguous circumstances

			 - Use your customers’ feedback to improve their experiences

			 - Proactively suggest creative solutions to meet your customers’ emerging needs

		 - Be focused: Scale for Tomorrow
			 - Take ownership for your work and consistently deliver on expectations

			 - Identify opportunities and make recommendations to simplify processes

			 - Ensure your day-to-day work aligns to your team or function’s big bets

			 - Consider trade-offs for different options before making decisions

		 - Be a leader: Inspire Others to Achieve
			 - Adapt your influencing style to strengthen your partnerships and engage your peers

			 - Ask for input from people across the matrix to inform your decisions

			 - Identify and build partnerships with key stakeholders across the matrix to achieve shared objectives

			 - Provide concise recommendations that include data, action steps and empathy to gain others’ commitment
