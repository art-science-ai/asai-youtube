---
title: Art and science PM Interview prep
---

- Why - mission
	 - Clarify: new product vs extension / timeline / scope

	 - State company mission

	 - Connect to company mission

	 - State product mission

	 - State product goal 
		 - Since this is high-level, start with engagement

- Who - users
	 - List different stakeholders: users / creators / businesses / advertisers / etc.

	 - Prioritize the consumer side - say others will follow

	 - Segment by dimensions: motivation / engagement level / demographics (life stage, money, time)

	 - Choose interesting user segment - frame as large groups with underserved needs

- What - user needs and pain points
	 - List user journey stages - before, during, after

	 - List needs AND pain points at each stage

	 - Prioritize by: mission, user chosen, severity

- Solutions
	 - List 3 solutions and describe in 2 sentences

	 - Prioritize by: mission, user chosen, pain point chosen

	 - Detail: entry point, main steps, critical action (optional: learning)

- Success metrics
	 - List metrics: adoption / engagement / retention

	 - Prioritize metric - usually engagement as %

	 - Metric downsides / trade-offs: doesn't track other stages of funnel / ecosystem health

- Conclusion
	 - Recap: mission > user > pain point > solution > metric

	 - Paint vision for future
