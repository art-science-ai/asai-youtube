		- Customers and markets: 2x2 product x customer matrix; market size, growth rate and segments

		- Company: mission, strategy, business model, assets, capabilities, brand, sales and marketing
		- Products
		- Industry trends
	- Framework - M&A
		- Acquiring company overview: mission, goals
		- Target company list of acquisition targets
		- Potential synergies
		- [Evaluation] - can those synergies be attained in other ways?
	- Framework - Industry list: TMT / HCLS / FSI / Consumer

		- Customer Purchase Decision Making Process: Need recognition, information search, evaluation of alternatives, purchase decision, post-purchase evaluation
		- Marketing Mix (4 Ps): product, price, promotion, place (distribution channels, etc.)
