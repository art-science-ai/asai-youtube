- W0: From product management to product leadership

	 - Need to shift from depth to breadth; need to shift from influencing specific product outcomes to a broad product strategy

		 - Feature adoption

		 - Feature retention

		 - Feature satisfaction

		 - Feature bundle evaluation

	 - Developing new features
		 - Identifying opportunities

		 - Prioritize high-impact features
