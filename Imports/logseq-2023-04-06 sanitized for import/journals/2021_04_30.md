- Chicken pulao #Recipe
	 - Ingredients
		 - Chicken, 1 lb

		 - Basmati rice, 1 cup

		 - Water, 1 cup

		 - Red onion, large, diced, 1/2

		 - Oil, 1 tbsp

		 - Ginger Garlic paste, 1 tbsp

		 - Lemon juice, 1 tbsp

		 - Salt, 1-2 tsp

		 - Dhaniya powder, 1.5 tsp

		 - Jeera powder, 1.5 tsp

		 - Chili powder, 1 tsp

		 - Garam masala, 1/2 tsp

	 - Procedure
		 - Marinate chicken with all the ingredients

		 - Saute onions in IP for 5-10 mins

		 - Add chicken and saute for 5 more mins

		 - Add rice, water, and stir.

		 - High pressure 6 mins + natural release (~ 20 mins) and keep warm.
