- Cashew chicken #Recipe
	 - Ingredients
		 - 1 kg chicken thighs boneless skinless (a.k.a. chicken thigh fillets)

		 - 3 or 4 tbsp Sambal Oelek chili paste

		 - 2 tbsp ginger garlic paste

		 - 2 tbsp olive oil

		 - 2 tbsp balsamic vinegar

		 - 1 or 2 tbsp honey

		 - 1/2 big lime freshly squeezed

		 - 2 tbsp corriander seed powder

		 - 1 tsp garam masala

		 - 1 or 2 big bunches of cilantro (use more than you think you need)

		 - 1 or 2 cups cashew halves

		 - Salt to taste

	 - Procedure
		 - Preheat oven to 375F / 190C.

		 - Place the dish in the oven, covered with foil.

		 - After 45 minutes, remove and stir well.

		 - Put it back into the oven uncovered (without the foil) for 15 minutes.

		 - Remove from oven.

		 - Garnish with some freshly chopped cilantro.

		 - Serve with hot basmati rice.
