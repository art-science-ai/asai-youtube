
## Intro
Artificial Intelligence (AI) is transforming the world in remarkable ways. Whether you're curious about AI, looking to enhance your productivity, or aiming to revolutionize your career or business, understanding and utilizing AI can open numerous opportunities. This guide will take you through three phases of the AI journey, detailing what you should learn and do at each stage.
