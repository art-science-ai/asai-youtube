# About Art and Science of AI {#about-art-and-science-of-ai}

[Art and Science of AI](https://artscienceai.substack.com/about) is a podcast and newsletter about the science of how AI works and the art of using AI to reimagine your life, business, and society. Hosted by Nikhil Maddirala (AI Product Manager) and Piyush Agarwal (AI Sales Executive), bringing you their expertise from world‚Äôs leading AI companies.

Our vision is to educate, inspire, and empower our audience by bridging the gap between the technical intricacies of AI and its practical applications in everyday life, business, and society. We do this by providing educational and practical content that demystifies AI technologies while showcasing innovative ways to leverage AI for personal and professional growth.

# Target audience {#target-audience}

We are currently targeting a broad range of audiences interested in AI from beginner general audiences to advanced professional audiences. Over time we hope to discover which audience we connect most with and focus on that niche.

1. **AI curious**: People who are curious about AI but do not necessarily want to pursue a career or in-depth study in the field. E.g. I want to learn about AI and what is possible.  
2. **AI enthusiasts**: People interested in how AI can improve their daily lives, increase productivity, or automate mundane tasks. E.g. I want to use AI to help me write better emails.  
3. **AI professionals**: People looking to enhance their career prospects by acquiring AI-related skills. E.g. I want to become an AI Product Manager.  
4. **Entrepreneurial and Innovation**: People who want to leverage AI for their existing businesses or startup ideas. E.g. I want to use AI to enhance my business.
