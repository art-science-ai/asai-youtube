## EIN (Employer Identification Number)

You need EIN for:

* Banking
* Stripe/PayPal
* W-9 for U.S. clients
* Taxes
* Contractor onboarding

### Steps to Get EIN Yourself (Free)

1. Visit IRS EIN site
2. Select **Single-Member LLC**
3. Enter:
   * SSN
   * Business address
   * Owner details
4. Download EIN Confirmation Letter (CP 575)

**Avoid paying a service.** It takes ~5 minutes.

# 6. Taxes & Annual Compliance

## Wyoming Requirements

* Annual Report: ~$60
* Registered Agent Renewal: ~$50–$125

## IRS Requirements (As a U.S. Permanent Resident)

* File **Schedule C** for business income
* Deduct business expenses:

  * Hardware (laptop, tablet, mic)
  * Software (ChatGPT, Claude, GitHub Copilot)
  * Home office
  * Internet
  * Travel
  * Subcontractors
  * Compute costs (AWS, GCP, etc.)

## No Separate Business Tax Return

* A single-member LLC is a **disregarded entity**
* Only included in your personal return

## Indian Tax Interaction

* All worldwide income must be reported in U.S.
* If taxed in India → you claim **Foreign Tax Credit (FTC)**
* If paid into your U.S. LLC → no Indian GST or compliances
