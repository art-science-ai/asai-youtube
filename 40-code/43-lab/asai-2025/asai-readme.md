## Overview

Art and Science of AI is a company with the following scope:
- AI education: helping people learn about AI through the Art and Science of AI podcast and newsletter
- Open source AI projects: publishing open source projects on Github including AI tools (e.g. Claude Code plugins), AI frameworks (e.g. Obsidian project management) and AI projects (e.g. AI music generation)
- AI mentorship: help people learn how to use AI and to build their own AI products and solutions.
- AI consulting: consulting engagements to build custom AI agents and workflows for businesses. 
- AI product development: development of AI based products
