- Before labor
	- Kegel exercises
	- Birth ball exercises
- General tips
	- Remember to **breathe and relax**
		- Deep breathing
		- Relaxation exercise (e.g. Shinzen style)
		- steadily - like when exercising / don't resist contractions
	- Explore various positions
	- Keep drinking water; eat less - homemade juices, white rice
- Labor
	- stage 1 - passive | 12 - 18 hrs
		- 5-30 mins / 30s / 0-3cm / 0-30%
		- Keep moving / walking / squatting 
	- stage 1 - active | 
		- 3-5m / 60s / 6-10cm
		- Massage sides of spine (not spine), hot/cold compress
	- stage 2 - pushing
		- Go to labor room
		- Brace while pushing - time with contractions
	- stage 3 - after birth
		- continue contractions
		- episiotomy - stitches
- Interventions
	- Forceps / Vacuum - in case baby is not coming out naturally / gets stuck; vacuum is used as suction device; forceps are used to pull the baby out 
- Duration
	- Roughly 2 nights
