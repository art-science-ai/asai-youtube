With select chords - works on michaeleskin.com but not Obsidian
`%%MIDI gchord b6` - this is the key line, it tells the editor to play the chord once every 6 beats, i.e. once every measure in this case
```music-abc
X: 3
T:Happy Birthday to You
M:3/4
L:1/8
Q:1/4=120
K:C
%%stretchlast true
%%MIDI gchord b6
G>G | "C"A2 G2 c2 |"G" B4 G>G | A2 G2 d2 |
"C" c4 G>G | g2 e2 c2 |"F" (B2 A2) f>f |
"G7" e2 c2 d2 | "C"c6 |]
```
