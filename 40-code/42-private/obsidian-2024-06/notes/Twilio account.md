# SMS & Voice Reminder Consent & Privacy Policy

## 1. Introduction

Welcome! This page explains how **Nikhil’s AI Reminder Service** collects your consent—either via SMS reply or via a Twilio-recorded voice call—what types of messages you’ll receive, and how to opt out.

---

## 2. Opt-In Methods

### A. Text-Based Opt-In

1. **Initial SMS Invitation**
    
    - You receive:
        
        > “Hi [Name], this is Nikhil’s AI Reminder Service. I’d like to send you SMS reminders for our upcoming appointment, tasks, or events. Reply **YES** to confirm or **STOP** to decline.”
        
2. **Your Reply**
    
    - Reply **YES** → you’re opted in.
        
    - Reply **NO** or **STOP** → you’re opted out; no further texts.
        
3. **Proof of Consent**
    
    - Your “YES” reply is logged with timestamp in Twilio’s message history.
        

---

### B. Twilio-Recorded Verbal Opt-In

1. **Triggering the Call**
    
    - After an in-person ask (or at any time), Nikhil’s system places an outbound call via Twilio.
        
2. **Consent Script**
    
    > **AI Assistant (auto-voice):**  
    > “Hello, this is Nikhil’s AI Reminder Service. We send text reminders for appointments, tasks, project updates, and events. To confirm you’d like to receive these SMS reminders, please say **‘YES’** now. To decline, say **‘NO’**.”
    
3. **Recording & Storage**
    
    - The entire call is recorded and time-stamped in Twilio as verifiable consent.
        

---

## 3. Message Types & Frequency

- **Meeting Reminders**: Texts 24 hrs and 1 hr before.
    
- **Task & Project Updates**: As deadlines approach or changes occur.
    
- **Event Notifications**: Up to 3 alerts (e.g. 24 hrs, 1 hr, 15 min prior).
    
- **Urgent Follow-Ups**: One-off messages for last-minute changes.
    

You’ll receive **no more than 3 texts per item** (meeting/task/event) and **on average ≤ 10 messages/month**.

---

## 4. Your Data & Privacy

- **Collected**: Phone number, opt-in record (SMS reply or call recording), reminder history.
    
- **Stored**: Encrypted in transit and at rest.
    
- **Shared**: Never—strictly one-to-one between Nikhil and you.
    

---

## 5. Opt-Out & Help

- **STOP** → unsubscribe from texts.
    
- **HELP** → receive instructions.
    
- To delete all data, email privacy@nikhil.ai; we’ll erase your profile within 5 business days.
