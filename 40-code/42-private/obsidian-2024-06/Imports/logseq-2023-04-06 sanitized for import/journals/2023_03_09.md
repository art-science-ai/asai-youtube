		- Facilitator: Map key players and the journey of information or processes

	- Prioritize problems
		- Team member: Vote on the top problems (each team member selects their top 3-5 problems)
		- Facilitator: Choose the top 3-10 most voted problems and formulate them as HMW (how might we) questions
	- Ideate solutions
		- Team members: Each team member generates as many solutions as possible (minimum 10 per team member; 1-2 sentences per solution)
	- Prioritize solutions
		- Team member: Vote on the top solutions (each team member selects their top 3 problems)
		- Facilitator: Choose the top 1-3 most voted solutions
	- Prototype 1-3 solutions
