- [[Art and science PM Interview prep]] - for [[PM Interview Prep]] - Product Sense Interview
	 - General tips
		 - Try to have fun with this

		 - Two key skills to demonstrate: structure and empathy

	 - 12 step framework
		 - Get on the same page
			 - Clarify, as needed

			 - Preview structure ahead

		 - Set stage
			 - Company mission

			 - Expound on mission

			 - State product mission

			 - State product objective

		 - What to build
			 - Users

			 - Pain points

			 - Solution

		 - Final considerations
			 - How to measure success

			 - Downsides of metric

			 - Summarize and paint vision

	 - Details on each setp
		 - Clarify
			 - New product vs. extension

			 - Timeline and scope

		 - Expound
			 - Connect mission to 

		 - Product objective
			 - Common objectives: Adoption / engagement / retention / monetization

			 - Usually focus on engagement: providing more value to users
				 - Also: engagement is a leading indicator of retention

		 - Users
			 - Stakeholders / sides of marketplace - prioritize users

			 - Dimensions
				 - Motivation

				 - Engagement level
					 - Power users vs casual

				 - Demographics
					 - Life stage

					 - Money

					 - Time

			 - Pick interesting users with interesting pain points / frame as large groups with underserved needs

		 - Pain points
			 - Needs AND pain points

			 - User journey steps: before, during, after

			 - Prioritization axes: severity, funnel stage, mission 

		 - Solutions
			 - Describe 2-3 solutions in 2-3 sentences each

			 - Prioritize 1 solution

			 - Detail: entry point, main steps, critical action

		 - Success metrics
			 - Options: adoption / engagement / retention

			 - Prioritize engagement - usually as a %

			 - List downsides of metric
				 - Doesn't tell full picture - doesn't track retention, adoption

				 - Doesn't tell you what's driving the metric - doesn't give funnel analysis

				 - Doesn't tell you ecosystem health - cannibalization

		 - Summarize
			 - Recap: mission > user > pain point > solution > metric

			 - Paint vision for future

- [[Mock PM Interview]] - silent - doctor search and visit
	 - Framework - Product design
		 - Intro - set up the framework
			 - Let me first discuss how I'd like to approach this problem.

			 - First, I'd like to talk about WHY we are building this product. What's the mission and goal?

			 - Then discuss WHO we are building this product for. Who are the users?

			 - Then discuss what are the user problems that we want this product to solve.

			 - Finally I'll discuss potential solutions for what to build.

			 - If there's more time at the end, we can discuss success metrics for this product.

		 - Clarify my understanding
			 - Clarify product understanding

			 - Who is building the product and where?

			 - Constraints: platform / geography
				 - Geo: US only

		 - Why / motivation and mission
			 - State company mission
				 - Meta: Give people the power to build community and bring the world closer together. 

			 - Connect product to company mission
				 - Healthcare is an area where people rely on friends and family

				 - Health problems often bring families and friends close together - so many estranged relationships

			 - State product mission
				 - **Empower people to find healthcare by leveraging the power of their communities**

			 - Set product objective: adoption / engagement / retention / monetization
				 - Default: Engagement is the best measure of how much value our product is providing to users.

		 - Who / users
			 - {{timer}}

			 - Stakeholders and sides of marketplace: users / creators / businesses / suppliers / partners / employees / etc.
				 - **Patients / users**

				 - Providers: doctors, clinics, hospitals

				 - Payers: insurers, employers, government, etc.

			 - For the sake of time, prioritize user side - because a great user experience will ultimately benefit all other stakeholders of the product

			 - Segment by dimensions: goals, needs, engagement level, features used, knowledge / sophistication / interest levels, demographics (life stage / money / time)
				 - Age: child / adult / elderly

				 - Type of condition: **Chronic** vs one-off

				 - Reason: Prevention vs cure

				 - Health-interest: high / low

			 - Prioritize by why - frame as large group with underserved need

		 - What / user problems
			 - Recap so far: mission > who

			 - User journey stages: before, during, after

			 - Needs, pain points, consequences, workarounds / competitors
				 - **Find doctors**
					 - Don't know which ones accept my insurance

					 - **Don't know which ones are good / highly recommended**

					 - Don't know which ones have depth of expertise

				 - Schedule appointment
					 - Hard to reach on the phone

					 - Don't have email / messenger

				 - Get care - establish treatment plan / Ongoing communication and monitoring
					 - Can't communicate with the doctor - don't have messaging app

					 - No good way to update status

					 - Measure improvement

			 - Prioritize: frequency, severity, who, and why

		 - What / solution ideas
			 - {{timer}}

			 - Ref
				 - AI/ML, AR/VR, metaverse, ambient computing

				 - Growth: Targeted reminders, gamification

				 - Social: connecting people, creating community, collaborating

			 - List 3+ solution ideas 
				 - **Discussion forum for chronic conditions - ask for recommendations**

				 - Directory - doctors with details, friend recommendations, reviews

				 - Recommended doctors - personalized recommendations

			 - Prioritize: what, who, why

			 - Risks and trade-offs: cannibalization, privacy, trust

		 - Solution deep dive
			 - {{timer}}

			 - Entry point
				 - FB App > healthcare tab > chronic conditions

			 - Main steps and call to action
				 - Follow this condition

				 - See links and information related to this condition

				 - Read discussions

				 - Ask a question and get recommendations

			 - After

		 - Success metrics
			 - {{timer}}

			 - List metrics: adoption / engagement / retention / monetization
				 - Adoption
					 - Click on healthcare tab

					 - Click on chronic condition

				 - Engagement
					 - Follow a condition

					 - View a discussion

					 - Comment on existing post

					 - Start a post

				 - Retention
					 - View / comment on a discussion month on month

			 - Prioritize metric: usually engagement %
				 - **% of all FB users viewing a discussion on a day**

			 - Risks and trade-offs: incomplete picture / can't diagnose funnel stages / ecosystem health

		 - End
			 - Recap: mission > user > pain point > solution > metric

			 - Paint vision for future
				 - This is just the starting point - we just picked one use case and MVP solution.

				 - The mission is much broader.
