- Daily diet checklist
	 - Fiber: 25g+
		 - 3+ servings of veggies

		 - 2+ servings legumes

	 - Protein: 100g+

	 - Carbs: limit
		 - <=2 servings of whole grains

		 - No added sugar

		 - No refined carbs

	 - Meal principles
		 - Eat only at defined mealtimes

		 - Pre-load meals with fiber + vinegar (+ protein + fat)

	 - Alcohol
		 - ?

	 - Log all food and alcohol

- Sources
	 - [[How not to diet]] rules
		 - Not allowed (except cheats)
			 - Refined grains - white rice, bread, pasta, etc.

			 - Added sugars

			 - Baked goods / anything made with flour

			 - Alcohol

		 - Allowed in limited quantities
			 - Whole grains, lentils, and beans (cumulative): 3 servings / day (NM) 1/day (RS) serving size is 0.25 cup dry and 0.5 cup cooked

			 - Flax / chia seeds: 1 serving / day

			 - Nuts: 1 serving / day

			 - Yogurt: 1 serving / day

			 - Non-veg: 1 serving / day (at most 2 days / week) (NM) 3 servings/day (RS) serving size is 3 oz cooked, 4 oz raw of meat/fish OR 2 whole eggs

			 - Oil / fat: limited quantity

		 - Allowed in unlimited quantities (recommended minimum)
			 - Berries: 1+

			 - Fruits: 3+

			 - Cruciferous vegetables: 1+

			 - Greens: 2+

			 - Other vegetables: 2+

		 - Cheat days / meals
			 - One cheat day per week

			 - When socializing- either it has to be a cheat day, or else no alcohol or cheat foods

	 - [[Glucose goddess]] rules
		 - walk after eating: 10-20 min walk within 30-60 mins after eating

		 - pre-load meals: fiber > protein + fat > vinegar > starches > sugar
