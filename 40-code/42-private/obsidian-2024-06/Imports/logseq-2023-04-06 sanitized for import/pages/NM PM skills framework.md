---
title: NM PM skills framework
---

- Product specific knowledge
	 - Product knowledge: product features, use-cases, demos, technical details

	 - User and customer knowledge: personas, pain-points

	 - Industry and domain knowledge: competitive landscape, industry trends

	 - Business and company knowledge: stakeholders (engineering, marketing, GTM, sales, finance), processes, data sources, culture

- PM skills
	 - Business strategy skills
		 - Define product vision and strategy

		 - Product roadmap

		 - Alignment with business objectives, GTM, marketing, etc.

	 - Customer validation and prioritization skills
		 - Customer interviews and feedback - qualitative and quantitative

		 - Translating customer feedback into product improvements / features.

		 - Validation and prioritization of roadmap based on feedback

		 - Data and analytics: anchoring decisions in data

	 - Technical skills
		 - Understanding engineering architecture and limitations

	 - Execution skills
		 - Collaborating with engineering and design to breakdown roadmaps into epics and stories.

		 - Collaborating with design and engineering on stories.

		 - Product owner role on scrum team: bringing the voice of customer to make prioritization and trade-off decisions with engineering.

	 - Evangelism skills (overlaps with communication skills)
		 - Communicating (the product vision, strategy, roadmap, and features) to customers, stakeholders, sales, etc.

		 - Representing company at conferences, customer calls, events, etc.

- Communication skills
	 - Presentation skills, writing, and oral.

	 - Meeting skills: setting agenda, following-up

	 - Representing company at conferences, customer calls, events, etc.

- People skills
	 - Team collaboration skills - collaborating with engineering, design, marketing

	 - Stakeholder management skills - getting alignment with key stakeholders

	 - Leadership skills

- Misc
	 - Project management skills: GTD
