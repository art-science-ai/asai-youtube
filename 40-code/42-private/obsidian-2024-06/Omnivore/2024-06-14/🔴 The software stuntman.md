## A cheatsheet for improving your content UX

By ‚Äúcontent‚Äù I mean writing, audio, or video created for online distribution and consumption ‚Äî stuff consumed on phones and laptops. **By ‚Äúcontent UX,‚Äù I mean how your audience experiences your content.**

User experience. Your users are your audience, and their experience is, well, every single touchpoint to do with your newsletter. Every page, button, and line of text on your website. Every detail about the newsletter itself, from subject line to word count to format.

Every detail of your newsletter could probably be better. That‚Äôs absolutely the case with _Creator Spotlight,_ if I‚Äôm being honest. But to think about it all at once is overwhelming and unhelpful. So, a simple piece of advice: **make a list of the top three details you think could be tweaked to improve the UX of your newsletter. Try to improve** _**just**_ _**one**_ **next time you send an issue.**
